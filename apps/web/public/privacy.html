Privacy / data sovereignty for AI

Your agent thinks privately.
Its memories are yours.

Clude is built on a simple premise: AI should serve individuals, not surveil them. Private inference via Venice AI. Client-side encrypted memory via Clude Brain. Verifiable on Solana. No logs. No data harvesting. No centralised control.

The separation of mind and state
"In a world of growing AI integration into our daily lives, it is crucial that humans are able to interact with machine intelligence without centralised surveillance and control."
Erik Voorhees · Venice AI

Centuries ago, we separated church from state. The cypherpunks separated language from state with encryption. Bitcoin separated money from state. Now we separate mind from state, ensuring no entity holds a monopoly over machine intelligence.
