/ 02 · Architecture

Three kinds of memory, working together.

Episodic

What happened, when.

Raw conversational moments stored with full context. Decay over time, resurface via similarity. Your agent remembers the exact exchange from three weeks ago.

"You mentioned you prefer Svelte on
Tuesday morning at 09:14."
Semantic

What you believe, always.

Consolidated facts extracted from episodes. Stable. Queryable. Shared across every conversation without bloating context windows.

"User prefers Svelte over React"
importance: 0.78 · decay: 0.95
Procedural

How you work, reliably.

Routines, formatting preferences, tone. Reinforced every time the agent observes the pattern again. Never has to be re-taught.

"Keep responses under 100 words
unless a task requires more detail"
